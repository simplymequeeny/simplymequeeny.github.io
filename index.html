WHAT WE DO

Animal healthcare has always played second fiddle when compared to the evolution of human health care. This gap in infrastructure has made access to animal health care complex and inefficient.

We at Manakn strive to fill this gap through innovation by leveraging advancements in Artificial Intelligence. Our state of the art pet management and practice automation solutions stand testament to the capabilities of AI in animal care.

Haachy is born.

Haachy aims to revolutionize pet care by creating an ecosystem that facilitates all the key stakeholders to have an active role in the pet`s well-being, by seamlessly connecting them through an open, transparent and easy to use channel.

Haachy functions as a Pet Management platform which aims to simplify the pet parenting process. Pet parents have access to animal care services, pet care information databases, easy access to your vet, pet medical records and a lot more at the click of a button.

In its enterprise solution avatar, Haachy helps automate clinical processes for Vets, by managing data efficiently. Haachy also enables Vets to connect to pet-parents(and their furry babies) like never before!

Who we are

Manakn Corp was incorporated in 2015 with a mission to infuse technology and innovation to improve animal care and well-being.

Manakn Corp. is a group of alumni and students from the University of Waterloo with over 20 years of combined experience in the field of Computer science engineering.

We strive to bring the novelty and the sophistications of Artificial Intelligence to improve the quality of the Animal Healthcare framework.

Why we do it

Being passionate animal lovers and dedicated pet owners, we experienced the animal health care industry first-hand and the engineer in us realized the technology gap that exists in this field.
